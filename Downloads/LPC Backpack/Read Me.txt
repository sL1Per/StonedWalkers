The straps should be placed on a layer below the pack. The fins should go on top of the jetpack.

The straps may not work over clothing that extends beyond the outline of the LPC base.

The backpacks and straps use ElizaWy's LPC clothing color palette. The jetpack uses the colors from Nila122's LPC Roman Armor.
